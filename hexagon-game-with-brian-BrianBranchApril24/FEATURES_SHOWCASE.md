## 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Early Game**: Focus on Hearts and Moves for survivability
2. **Mid Game**: Add Damage cells once you can dodge well
3. **Late Game**: Specialize into a synergy build
4. **Boss Waves**: Build defensively before waves 10, 20, 30, etc.
5. **Abilities**: Save Burst for crowds, Dash for emergencies
6. **Meta**: Invest in XP Gain early for faster progression
7. **Synergies**: Experiment! Some combos are incredibly powerful
