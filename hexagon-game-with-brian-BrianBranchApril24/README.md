# HEXCORE: ASCEND 🔷

A roguelike cell-building survival game inspired by Primordialis. Build your organism from hexagonal cells, unlock powerful synergies, and survive increasingly deadly waves of enemies!

## 🎮 Features

### Core Gameplay
- **Wave-Based Survival**: Fight through 50 waves of increasingly difficult enemies
- **Cell Building System**: Customize your organism with 14 unique cell types
- **Active Abilities**: Trigger powerful abilities like AOE bursts and dashes
- **Boss Battles**: Face challenging bosses every 10 waves with unique mechanics
- **Synergy System**: Discover powerful combinations between cell types

### Cell Types
- **Heart** ♥ - Increases max HP, respawns when destroyed
- **Move** ➤ - Boosts movement speed for dodging
- **Damage** ✦ - Deals contact damage to nearby enemies
- **Shield** ⬡ - Reduces incoming damage
- **Regen** ✚ - Passive healing over time
- **Spike** ⚡ - Reflects damage back to attackers
- **Magnet** ◉ - Pulls XP and powerups from afar
- **Burst** 💥 - Active AOE damage ability
- **Dash** ⚡ - Quick dodge with invulnerability
- **Leech** 🩸 - Lifesteal from damage dealt
- **Toxic** ☠ - Applies poison damage over time
- **Crystal** 💎 - Increases XP gain (fragile)
- **Void** 🌀 - Chance to dodge attacks completely

### Meta-Progression
- **Persistent Unlocks**: Unlock new cell types by reaching higher waves
- **Essence Currency**: Earn essence from runs to purchase permanent upgrades
- **Permanent Upgrades**: Boost starting stats, XP gain, cell rewards, and more
- **Achievements**: Complete challenges to unlock rewards
- **Statistics Tracking**: Track your total runs, kills, and high scores

### Enhanced UI
- Modern, polished interface with smooth animations
- Real-time ability cooldown indicators
- Boss health bars for epic encounters
- Floating damage numbers and score popups
- Synergy panel showing active bonuses
- Gradient health bars with color-coded warnings

## 🎯 Controls

### Movement
- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** - Move your organism
- **Mouse** - Navigate menus

### Abilities
- **1** - Activate first ability (if available)
- **2** - Activate second ability (if available)

### Build Phase
- **Left Click** - Cycle through cell types
- **Right Click** - Remove cell
- **Launch Button** - Start next wave

## 🏆 Progression

### Unlocking Cells
- Wave 3: Regen Cell
- Wave 5: Spike Cell
- Wave 7: Magnet Cell
- Wave 10: Burst Cell (+ First Boss!)
- Wave 12: Dash Cell
- Wave 15: Leech Cell
- Wave 18: Toxic Cell
- Wave 20: Crystal Cell (+ Second Boss!)
- Wave 25: Void Cell

### Boss Waves
Every 10th wave spawns a powerful boss with:
- Multiple phases that change behavior
- Massive health pools
- Unique attack patterns
- Special abilities per phase

## 💡 Tips

1. **Balance is Key**: Mix offensive and defensive cells for survivability
2. **Synergies Matter**: Certain cell combinations provide massive bonuses
3. **Abilities Win Fights**: Use Burst to clear crowds, Dash to escape danger
4. **Protect Your Heart**: Heart cells respawn, but you're vulnerable while they're down
5. **Farm Early Waves**: Maximize XP and cell collection in easier waves
6. **Boss Preparation**: Build defensively before boss waves (10, 20, 30, etc.)
